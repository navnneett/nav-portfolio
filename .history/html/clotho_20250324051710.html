Clotho

WebApp | UI/UX Design, Frontend

Clotho is a clothing management and AI-generated outfit suggestions app, designed to address common wardrobe challenges.

Project Role

UI/UX Designer, Frontend Developer

Timeline

2 months

Tools

Next.js, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma

DESIGN RATIONALE

Clotho is designed to tackle overbuying and forgotten outfits by helping users manage their wardrobe efficiently. The vibrant UI featuring cool tones like blues, pinks, and violets, evoke a sense of creativity, calmness, and inspiration. A dapper mascot cat assists users, offering outfit combos, personalized recommendations, and ideal style suggestions based on needs. Color-coded sections and visually appealing icons help users navigate effortlessly, making wardrobe organization fun rather than tedious.

Clotho tailors looks based on weather, occasions, and personal preferences, ensuring both practicality and style. By making wardrobe management fun and interactive, it encourages mindful fashion choices and smarter shopping habits.

Civica

Legal Assistance App | UI/UX Design, Frontend

Civica is an app designed to assist individuals with navigating legal challenges and getting affordable access to legal services and resources.

Timeline

6 Weeks

DESIGN RATIONALE

Civica was created to fill the gap in legal support for underprivileged women, particularly those facing complex legal challenges. Its design focuses on providing accessible, affordable, and easy-to-use resources that simplify legal processes, offer emotional support, and empower women to confidently navigate their rights. The app ensures that vulnerable women can access the legal protections they deserve, offering a user-friendly and trustworthy platform for support.

The design focuses on accessibility, clarity, and ease of use, providing users with a seamless experience when seeking legal support. With careful attention to color psychology and intuitive navigation, the app aims to reduce the stress of navigating legal processes and encourage positive engagement.

User Research

  • Ideation

The primary persona is a single mother navigating workplace harassment and family law challenges. The secondary persona is a woman facing financial struggles, needing help with unpaid wages and employment claims.

The case studies conducted with an immigration lawyer in BC highlight various legal challenges faced by vulnerable women in Canada. These include workplace exploitation, domestic abuse, marriage fraud, harassment in rental situations, and unfair treatment during employment training.

Through these real-life examples, Civica aims to address these issues by offering educational resources, legal support, and emotional guidance to empower women in navigating complex legal and personal situations.

Our competitor analysis and legal process research, based on common case studies, identify gaps in existing legal resources for vulnerable women. By examining real-life challenges like workplace exploitation, harassment, and legal ignorance, we aim to develop a solution that provides accessible, clear, and supportive legal guidance tailored to these needs.

In the ideation phase, we focused on creating a logo that symbolizes compassion and support, using two connected hearts. All drafts centered around this theme, with blue tones to evoke trustworthiness, as reflected in user surveys. The goal was to design a visual identity that resonated with our mission of offering accessible, supportive legal help for women.

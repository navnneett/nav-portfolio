Dance Party!

Interactive Children's E-book

The story follows the squirrel's desire to stand out at the party, exploring the importance of inner beauty through a humorous adventure of trying on extravagant features inspired by her animal friends, like wings, springy legs, stripes, and feathers. Ultimately, she learns from her mother that true fancy comes from being herself.

Timeline

2 Weeks

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Figma

DESIGN RATIONALE

The motivation behind this project stems from a personal connection to childhood memories of dressing up and pretending to be something special. The story emphasizes self-acceptance, the value of kindness, and the importance of feeling beautiful in your own skin.
